Continue to learn about Joe Hisaishi,
the composer behind the studio's music.

Studio Ghibili's composer, was born in Nakano, Japan, as Mamoru Fujisawa. His first instrument was violin, which he learnt as a four year old at the Violin School Suzuki Shinichi. He later attended college in 1969 where he majored in music composition. After his studies, as he became better known he made Joe Hisaishi his professional alias. In 1985 he founded his recording studio, and one year later begin collaberative work with Studio Ghilbli.
