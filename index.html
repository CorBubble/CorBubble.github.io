Studio Ghibli is Japanese animation studio based in Tokyo. They have created many beloved films such as My Neighbor Totoro, Howl's Moving Castle and The Secret World Of Arrietty. This website will explore Studio Ghibli's history, music, animation style, and films. Continue reading the main page for some key dates of the studio's history.

    Totoro

  • February 17, 2010: Release date of Arrietty
